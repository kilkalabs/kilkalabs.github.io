Support

How can I get help with my app?

Our support team is available via email at support@kilkalabs.com. We typically respond within 1-2 business days.

What information should I include when reporting an issue?

Please include the app name, version number, your device model and operating system version, and a detailed description of the issue, including any error messages and steps to reproduce the problem.
